About the Department of CSE

Excellence in education, innovation, and research.

Welcome to the Department of CSE

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at the University of Barishal is committed to delivering world-class education and research opportunities in computing and technology. Since its establishment, the department has been dedicated to producing graduates who excel in both industry and academia.

With a strong emphasis on software development, artificial intelligence, data science, and networking, our curriculum is designed to meet the needs of the modern tech-driven world. The department also promotes research and innovation through advanced laboratories and projects.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education in computer science and engineering, fostering innovation, creativity, and leadership skills among students.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in computer science education and research, preparing students to contribute globally to technology and society.

History

The Department of CSE at the University of Barishal started its academic journey in recent years with the aim of promoting computer education in southern Bangladesh. Despite being relatively new, the department has grown rapidly with dedicated faculty members and enthusiastic students. Our graduates are already making their mark in leading industries and institutions across the country.
